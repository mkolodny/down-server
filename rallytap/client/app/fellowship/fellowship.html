The Rallytap Fellowship

Learn how to market an app.

{% endverbatim %} {% verbatim %}

High School Students

Learn how to market digital products from a lecturer at Purdue University who lead a user acquisition team at a $70M company. You will learn cutting-edge digital marketing strategies. And you will get to apply what you learn by marketing a real app.

Format

The fellowship will consist of three 2-week cycles. Each cycle will include: learning a new skill/tools, developing a marketing plan, getting feedback, trying out your plan, and collecting data.

  • Starts November 11th, 2015
  • Lasts 6 weeks
  • 1 day/week after school

What You Will Learn

  • User Acquisition
    • Marketing framework
    • Creating KPIs
    • Campaign tracking
  • User Experience
    • User Studies
    • Quantitative Feedback
  • Engagement
    • Using data to understand behavior
    • Identifying drop-off
    • Re-engagment

How to Apply

To apply, download the app Rallytap, and enter your username and school below. On November 11th, 2015, we will select one to three students per school to be part of the fellowship. Fellows will be selected based on how many friends they add on Rallytap, and how many of their friends download the app.
